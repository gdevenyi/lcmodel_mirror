CONTIN-CD

Applications:

  • This version of CONTIN is specialized to the estimation of protein secondary structure content from far-uv circular dichroism. It has been applied by others to data from Raman and IR spectra.
  • It is running in hundreds of laboratories with a wide variety of computers.

Reference (in addition to those for CONTIN):

  • S.W. Provencher & J. Glöckner: Estimation of globular protein secondary structure from circular dichroism. Biochemistry 20, 33 (1981).

Downloading:

Download by clicking on the 7 files below and saving them from your browser.
  1. README.txt (1.3KB): Short guide for the installation and test run; print this first.
  2. contin-manual1.pdf (4292kB);
  3. contin-manual2.pdf (1960kB);
  4. cpc1.pdf (1009kB) (useful, but not essential reading);
  5. cpc2.pdf (640kB) (useful, but not essential reading);
  6. contin-cd.for ( 81KB): (zipped with gzip) Fortran source code (not needed if you can use one of the two executables listed below).
  7. contin-cd.in (561 B): Test data.
You need a Fortran compiler, except that the following (gzip'd) executable may work on your Linux system: contin-cd-linux (112KB),
or the following executable may work on your MS Windows system: contin-cd-windows.exe (640KB).
